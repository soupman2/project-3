in Order

  1. clean carpet
  2. do dishes
  3. dry clothes

How to do

Dry clothes:Hang them on the drying rack. Do not hang if rainy now or later. Fold and stack them after they are all dry.

Clean carpet:Hang it outside and whack it with a bat. Use a vacuum cleaner afterwards. Place back after cleaned

Do dishes:Put as much as you can in the dishwasher then scrub the rest with sponge or steel wool. Stack all the clean plates
